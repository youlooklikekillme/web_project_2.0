GMO-Free Policy

At "Japan Sushi," we take great care in selecting high-quality, natural ingredients to ensure that every dish is safe and healthy for our guests. We are committed to using non-GMO (Genetically Modified Organism) products, ensuring that our customers receive only the freshest and most natural ingredients.

Our menu features a variety of options made from non-GMO rice, vegetables, and seafood sourced from trusted suppliers. You can rest assured that every meal at "Japan Sushi" reflects our dedication to quality and purity.

Health & Safety Standards

The health and safety of our guests and staff is our top priority. At "Japan Sushi," we adhere to the highest standards of hygiene and safety protocols to ensure a clean and safe environment for everyone.

Our restaurant undergoes regular health inspections, and we comply with all local health and safety regulations. We maintain rigorous cleanliness procedures in all areas, from food preparation to dining spaces.

We also ensure that our staff is well-trained in food safety practices and follows best practices for personal hygiene.

Environmental Responsibility

At "Japan Sushi," we are dedicated to reducing our environmental impact through sustainable practices. We prioritize the use of eco-friendly packaging, minimize food waste, and source ingredients from local and environmentally conscious suppliers.

Our restaurant is committed to conserving energy and reducing water consumption, ensuring a more sustainable approach to our daily operations.

By choosing "Japan Sushi," you’re supporting a restaurant that values environmental responsibility and contributes to a greener future.

About "Japan Sushi"

"Japan Sushi" was founded with a passion for authentic Japanese cuisine and a commitment to providing guests with a unique dining experience. Our mission is to blend traditional flavors with modern creativity, offering a menu that caters to both Japanese food enthusiasts and those looking to explore new tastes.

Located in the heart of Nizhny Novgorod, near the picturesque Volga embankment, we aim to be a go-to destination for those seeking high-quality Japanese cuisine.

Our Story

"Japan Sushi" was established with a vision to bring the authentic flavors of Japan to Nizhny Novgorod. The idea was born from a deep passion for Japanese culinary traditions and a desire to create a restaurant that embodies the essence of Japanese culture.

From our carefully curated interior to our carefully sourced ingredients, we strive to offer an exceptional dining experience that reflects the rich heritage of Japanese cuisine.
