Welcome to "Japan Sushi"

— a unique restaurant where traditional Japanese cuisine blends with modern design and a cozy atmosphere. Our restaurant is located in the heart of Nizhny Novgorod, near the picturesque Volga embankment, making it the perfect place for romantic dinners and meetings with friends.

Interior:

Upon entering "Japan Sushi," you will be greeted by a stylish interior decorated in Japanese style. Warm tones, natural materials, and Japanese decor elements create a harmonious atmosphere. Cozy wooden partitions and soft lighting add intimacy, making every visit special. On the walls, you can see paintings by Japanese masters, as well as frescoes depicting traditional Japanese landscapes.

Menu:

Our menu offers a wide selection of sushi, rolls, sashimi, and original Japanese dishes made from fresh, high-quality ingredients. You can try both classic options and chef's signature combinations that will delight even the most discerning gourmets. We also have vegetarian and gluten-free options so that every guest can find something for themselves.

Location:

"Japan Sushi" is conveniently located at 48 Bolshaya Pokrovskaya Street, right in the city center. You can get to us by public transport or car, and there is parking nearby. We invite you to visit "Japan Sushi" and enjoy authentic Japanese cuisine in a unique atmosphere. If you are looking for a place to relax and treat yourself to high-quality food, our restaurant will be the perfect choice for you!
