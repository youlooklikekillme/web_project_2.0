Peasant Set

Simple yet incredibly delicious, this set reflects the spirit of Japanese tradition. It features popular rolls with crab, cucumber, and salmon, perfect for everyday meals or heartfelt gatherings. A great choice for those who value freshness and natural flavors.

Shogun Set

A luxurious set fit for a great ruler. It combines premium ingredients like eel, red caviar, and cream cheese with exquisite rolls and sushi. Ideal for special occasions or to indulge in a royal feast on any day.

Samurai Set

A perfect choice for those who appreciate tradition and harmony. This set includes classic rolls and sushi, crafted with the precision of a samurai's blade. The magnificence of fresh fish, avocado, and delicate rice in every bite offers an authentic taste of Japan.

Ninja Roll Collection

A mysterious and sophisticated set that combines the sharp flavors of wasabi, the tender textures of avocado, and secret ingredients, much like the hidden techniques of a ninja.

Daimyo Feast

A luxurious set worthy of a Japanese feudal lord. Features premium ingredients like red caviar, scallops, and eel.

Ronin Set

For those who value freedom of choice and independence. This set includes a variety of sushi and rolls, reflecting the path of a wandering samurai.

Bamboo Spirit

Inspired by the freshness of Japanese forests, this soup includes shiitake mushrooms, bamboo shoots, and a light soy-based broth, offering natural serenity with every spoonful.

Hikari Harmony

Experience the gentle warmth of traditional Japan with "Hikari Harmony." A classic blend of miso paste, silky tofu, wakame seaweed, and green onions creates the perfect balance of flavor and aroma. This soup offers comfort in every sip.

Kyoto Comfort

A refined combination of aromatic chicken broth, noodles, and vegetables creates a harmony of flavors reminiscent of cozy evenings in Kyoto. Add a soft-yolk egg and caramelized onions for complete satisfaction.
